<p>It's here! I finally republished ascii.garden after months of not doing it after I said I would!</p>
<p>This time, the site is built with one of the off-the-shelf static site generators (currently, 11ty). The result of this choice was a refactor that is in conflict with my very soul. In all ways, as a person, I am wholly against solving a problem with a 10,000 dollar multitool that should be solved with a hammer and a knife. I also hate JavaScript so so so much despite my past attempts to get over this. Inside me there are two wolves and one is a unique kind of software elitist I cannot kill. </p>
<p>Regardless, my priority here was to make it as painless as possible to actually post. It is easy. And the tags work. It took an afternoon and the result is a mostly unobjectionable static site. So despite being possessed by a wolf that screams "oh my god I hate npm packages and apps" every time I breathe in the direction of the web, we're doing this. For my friends that are interested in starting up blogs, I think I can recommend this method maybeeeee possibly.</p>
<p>The content here is so far the same as it was after the last refactor, but I've deleted things. In 2025, I essentially only published content I had to write half-asleep for a grade at school (both posts and resume revisions)! No surprise, these were bad! Also, some one-offs have been deleted i.e. the fram2 test keps. If you need these for whatever reason feel free to email me.</p>
<p>I'm excited to fill this site with tons of new stuff. Pictures of places I've been, logs about things I'm working on, art, ramblings and rantings, etc. Should be fun!!</p>
